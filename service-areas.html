Greater Houston Area

Houston is the most diverse housing market in Texas — from historic Craftsman bungalows in the Heights to luxury high-rises in the Galleria area to sprawling new developments in Katy and Sugar Land. The humidity and heat in Houston create specific challenges for drywall and plaster: moisture-resistant board in appropriate areas, proper ventilation during cure, and attention to substrate conditions. We know Houston construction and we work across the full metro area.
