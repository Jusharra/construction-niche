The Valley Wall Doctor coordinates experienced drywall hangers who hang, tape, mud, and deliver walls that are smooth, level, and ready for whatever finish you choose — paint, texture, tile, or wallpaper.

Frequently Asked Questions

Drywall installation pricing varies based on square footage, board type, ceiling height, and finish level. In the DFW area, expect roughly $1.50–$3.50 per square foot for hanging and finishing, depending on complexity. We provide detailed, itemized estimates at no charge so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

A standard room (12×12) can be hung and taped in a day. A full home installation typically takes 3–7 days for hanging, plus additional days for taping, mudding, and drying between coats. Commercial projects vary based on scope. We'll give you a realistic timeline with your estimate.

For wet or high-humidity areas like bathrooms, laundry rooms, and utility spaces, we use moisture-resistant drywall (greenboard) or cement board for tile substrates. Standard drywall will fail quickly in these environments. We'll recommend the right product for each area of your project.

Permits are typically required for new construction, room additions, and projects involving fire-rated or structural assemblies. Simple remodel work replacing like-for-like drywall generally does not require a permit. We can help you navigate permit requirements for your specific city — DFW and Houston area municipalities each have different rules. See our Permit Handling service for details.
