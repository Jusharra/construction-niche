Professional Drywall Repair in Texas

Drywall damage happens — doorknob holes, settling cracks, water stains, failed tape seams, nail pops, and damaged corners are everyday issues in homes and commercial properties across the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex and Greater Houston. The challenge isn't just patching the hole; it's making the repair invisible. That's where texture matching becomes critical and where most DIY attempts fall short.

The Valley Wall Doctor coordinates skilled repair technicians who assess the damage, determine the right repair method, and match your existing texture — whether it's orange peel, knockdown, skip trowel, or smooth — so the repaired area blends seamlessly with the surrounding wall. For water-damaged areas, we also assess whether moisture is still present and advise on addressing the root cause before closing the wall.

We handle drywall repair jobs of all sizes across DFW and Houston — from a single doorknob hole to full wall sections damaged by flooding or remodel work. If you're in Dallas, Plano, Katy, Sugar Land, or anywhere in between, we can get a crew out fast and a written estimate to you within 1 business day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cosmetic damage — nail pops, small holes, surface cracks — doesn't affect the structural integrity of your walls. Structural concerns involve cracks that run diagonally from window or door corners, wide gaps that recur after repair, or walls that bow or flex. If you're seeing recurring cracks or unusual wall movement, we'll assess the situation and let you know whether it's a drywall issue or something that needs a structural engineer's review first.
