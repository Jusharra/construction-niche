Fast, Detailed Drywall Estimates in Texas

Getting a drywall estimate shouldn't take a week. At The Valley Wall Doctor, we turn around written estimates within 1 business day of reviewing your project — by phone, by photos, or in person for larger jobs. Our estimates are itemized and specific: you'll know exactly what work is included, what the materials are, and what you're paying for. No vague "starting at" quotes.

We estimate drywall installation (new construction and remodels), drywall repair (small patches to whole-room work), finishing (Level 3 through Level 5), texturing, plastering, and popcorn ceiling removal. We also scope full projects for GCs, property managers, and investors who need pricing across multiple trades or properties.

For general contractors and remodelers, we offer fast-turnaround bid packages that include material quantities, labor breakdown, and timeline estimates — formatted for your project budget or bid submission. If you're working on a permit-required project, we can coordinate the drywall scope with the permit requirements. See our Project Coordination service for larger build-outs.
